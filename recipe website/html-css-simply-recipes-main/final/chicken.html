chicken stir-fry with vegetables

This recipe is versatile, so feel free to customize it by adding your favorite vegetables or adjusting the seasonings to suit your taste.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dietfood

instructions

Step1

Start by marinating the thinly sliced chicken breasts. In a mixing bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Add the sliced chicken to the bowl and toss until evenly coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 15-20 minutes. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

Step2

Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken slices to the skillet. Stir-fry the chicken for 5-6 minutes or until it is cooked through and slightly browned. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside on a plate. In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed, then add the sliced onions, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and broccoli florets. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes or until they are tender-crisp. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Stir everything together to combine.

Step3

Season the stir-fry with salt and pepper to taste. You can adjust the seasoning according to your preference. Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove the skillet from the heat. Serve the chicken stir-fry hot over cooked rice. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious chicken stir-fry!

ingredients

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon oyster sauce

1 tablespoon hoisin sauce

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon ginger, minced

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced 1 onion, thinly sliced

1 cup broccoli florets Salt and pepper to taste

Cooked rice, for serving

tools

Large skillet or wok

Wooden spoon or spatula

Cutting board

Knife

Mixing bowls
