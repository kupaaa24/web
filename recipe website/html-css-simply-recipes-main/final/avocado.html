avocado toast recipe

This recipe is not only quick and easy to make but also highly customizable, allowing you to get creative with your toppings based on what you have on hand or your personal preferences.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dietfood

instructions

Step1:

Start by toasting your slices of whole grain bread until they reach your desired level of crispiness. While the bread is toasting, halve the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a small bowl.

Step2

Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency. You can leave it slightly chunky or make it smooth, depending on your preference. Season the mashed avocado with salt and pepper to taste, and mix well to incorporate the seasoning evenly.

Step3:

Once the bread is toasted, spread the mashed avocado evenly onto each slice. If desired, add your choice of toppings on top of the avocado. Some popular options include sliced cherry tomatoes, a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for heat, thinly sliced radishes for crunch, a poached egg for protein, crumbled feta cheese for tanginess, or a handful of fresh microgreens for freshness. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious and nutritious avocado toast!

ingredients

2 slices of whole grain bread

1 ripe avocado

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, sliced radishes, poached egg, feta cheese, or microgreens
